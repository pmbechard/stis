ABOUT US

We have a history of serving international students and families in Shenyang for over a quarter of a century. We were founded in 1998 and have been putting seniors in colleges and universities around the world ever since. We currently have students and staff from 5 continents from countries like Australia, Canada, China, Japan, Korea, Russia, South Africa, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
From our students and their families to our staff and their families, our core values are focused on transforming young lives through a wide variety of teaching and learning opportunities.
We are a College Board approved testing center for SAT and AP exams. We have a full time college counselor on staff. Our graduates have advanced to college for over 25 years. Our 21st Century teaching includes computer science courses for all grade levels. Courses range from grade 1 hands on orientation in the computer lab to exposure to Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, to AP Level coding, programming, and writing games and APPs.

ACADEMICS

What We Offer

Our kindergarten students receive love and care with learning ongoing through all they do. All traditional kindergarten content is presenting through singing, dancing, laughter, repeating, and doing things. Our International and Chinese National kindergarten staff are nurturing and caring teachers.
The core of our elementary curriculum is using the Wonders program for English and the Fusion program for science. Social Studies, customs, and culture are built into these two foundations. Teachers use their vast prior teaching experience to supplement and enhance their teaching. All elementary classes have weekly opportunities in music, art, physical education, library, and computer lab. Weekly fun assemblies, field trips, projects, and special events provide learning opportunities in fun ways.
